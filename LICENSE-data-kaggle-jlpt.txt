Changes Made (CC BY 4.0 §3(a)(1)(B) — indication of modifications)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The following modifications have been made to the original dataset:

  1. Selected curated vocabulary per JLPT level
     (editor curation — the source dataset does not include a frequency column):
       N5:   300 words
       N4:   600 words
       N3: 1,100 words
       N2: 1,700 words
       N1: 2,500 words
       Total: 6,200 words
  2. Added Korean translations (meaning_ko). Generated as drafts via
     OpenAI GPT-4o and then reviewed and corrected by a human editor.
     Final translations are the editor's work product, not GPT output.
  3. Added card-type classification (A/B/C/D/E) for UI rendering
  4. Added part-of-speech metadata
  5. Added example sentences (sourced separately from Tatoeba, CC BY 2.0 FR)
  6. Removed English meanings from the user-facing app (retained for QA only)
  7. Re-indexed with internal IDs

All five JLPT levels (N1-N5) are sourced from this single Kaggle dataset.
No additional data sources are used for vocabulary masters.
